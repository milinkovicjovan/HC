Our Origin Story

Meet Sasha, founder of Vega IT, who felt the rhythm of his company fade as it grew from 3 to 800 employees. Feeling a disconnect, HeartCount was built as a lifeline to bridge the gap and nurture the well-being of his employees. Today, HeartCount supports companies globally that prioritize employee engagement and cultivate connections throughout their organizations.

Sasa Popovic

CEO and co-founder of VegaIT

Our purpose

Help people be seen, heard, and valued, creating organizations where people feel they belong and know they matter.

Dusan Milicevic

GM at Comtrade System Integration

"The Customizable Questions allowed us to focus on specific aspects crucial to our team's success. By implementing Employee's Suggestions gathered through HeartCount we made CTSI a better place to work."

The vision

A world where healthy organizations create happy employees, who contribute to a happier world.

The mission

Build a growing, sustainable movement of organizations who create positive, lasting, people- focused cultures by counting hearts, not heads.

Olga Arsov

The Semi-Annual Report offers a comprehensive summary of your company’s status over the two halves of each year, focusing on well-being, engagement, and employee feedback.

This report details the statistics and scores for each employee experience category surveyed during a six month period. It also includes an in-depth analysis of every survey question.

The Summary Page is your starting point when you log into the portal

On the Summary Page you'll find key information about the current Pulse Check survey and predefined periods for analyzing survey data over time (monthly, quarterly, semi-annually, and annually).
